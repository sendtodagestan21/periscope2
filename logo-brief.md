## The Brand Metaphor

A periscope allows you to see clearly above the surface while submerged.  
Business owners are underwater in operations — we give them visibility.

The logo must carry this feeling: **clarity, elevation, always-on awareness.**

---

## Logo System Required

### 1. Primary Logo (Horizontal)
- Icon mark + "PERISCOPE" wordmark side by side
- Primary use: website header, email signatures, business cards

### 2. Stacked Logo
- Icon mark above wordmark
- Use: app icons, social avatars, square contexts

### 3. Icon Mark Only
- The standalone periscope symbol
- Use: favicon (16×16, 32×32), app icon, loading screens

### 4. White Reverse
- White-only version of all above
- Use: dark backgrounds (navy, dark blue, gradient)

### 5. Monochrome
- Single color (black or white)
- Use: embossing, embroidery, single-color print

## Icon Mark Concepts

### Concept A — The Periscope Tube (Recommended)
A minimal geometric periscope shape: vertical tube with a 90° elbow at top, with a subtle lens/eye aperture. The aperture incorporates a small signal pulse (3 curved lines, like wifi symbol) indicating communication.

The key insight: the periscope "sees" AND "communicates."

```
    ╔═══╗  ← Lens aperture with signal waves
    ║   ║  ← Viewing prism
    ║   ║
    ║   ║  ← Main tube
    ╚═══╝
```

Modernized: Clean geometric rectangles, rounded corners (radius 4px relative), the signal waves emanating from the top lens as 2–3 curved arcs.

### Concept B — The P Mark
A lettermark where the "P" bowl opens upward like a periscope head, with a small signal dot at the apex. Clean, corporate, versatile.

### Concept C — Lens Aperture
An abstract camera/periscope lens aperture (hexagonal iris shape) with clean geometric precision. Suggests: focus, clarity, vision. The aperture blades incorporate subtle signal wave lines.

## Wordmark Specifications

**Font:** Space Grotesk (or custom geometric sans)  
**Weight:** Bold (700)  
**Case:** ALL CAPS  
**Tracking:** +100 (wide letter spacing)  
**The "I" modification:** The dot above lowercase "i" — if we use mixed case or stylized — could be replaced with the lens icon. If all-caps, the vertical bar of the "I" could have a small lens mark at the top.

**Wordmark only (no icon):** PERISCOPE  
**With tagline:** PERISCOPE / AI COMMUNICATION PLATFORM (in smaller Inter Regular)

---

## What to Avoid

- ❌ No drop shadows on the logo
- ❌ No outlines/strokes around the icon when on white
- ❌ No gradients on the wordmark
- ❌ No lowercase wordmark
- ❌ No decorative serifs
- ❌ No "techy" circuit board patterns incorporated into mark
- ❌ No literal submarine imagery
- ❌ No blue ocean waves (too cliché for this brand)

---

## Competitive Reference

Look at: Stripe, Linear, Notion, OpenAI — all share: geometric precision, minimal forms, strong negative space, confident proportions.

What we want to feel like: **Stripe** (trustworthy, premium, modern) meets **Linear** (clean, technical, confident) — but with our own periscope identity baked in.

---

## Size & Clear Space

**Minimum size (icon):** 16px × 16px  
**Minimum size (full logo):** 120px wide  
**Clear space:** Equal to the height of the "P" letterform on all four sides  
**Safe zone:** No other visual elements within the clear space
