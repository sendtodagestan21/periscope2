What Happens After You Sign Up

✅
Step 1 · Complete

Payment Confirmed

Your account is set up and a receipt has been sent to your email. Welcome to Company Periscope.

📋
Step 2 · You Are Here

Complete the Intake Form

Fill out the form below — your business info, hours, services, AI personality, and call handling preferences. This takes about 10–15 minutes and trains your AI to sound exactly like your team.

🤖
Step 3 · Within 24–48 Hours

We Build Your AI

Our team configures and trains your AI receptionist on your specific business, scripts, and call flows. You'll receive a test call to approve before anything goes live.

📞
Step 4 · Go Live

Your AI Starts Answering

We set up call forwarding and your AI takes over — answering every call, booking appointments, and capturing leads around the clock. You'll have dashboard access to monitor everything in real time.

📊
Step 5 · Day 7

Review & Optimize

We review your first week of call logs together and fine-tune anything that needs adjusting. You're never on your own — ongoing support is always included.

Have These Ready

The form below takes 10–15 minutes. Having this info handy speeds things up.

🏢

Business Basics

  • Business name, address, and phone number
  • Website URL
  • Business hours (including holidays)
  • Owner or office manager contact
📋

Services & Pricing

  • Complete list of services you offer
  • Pricing ranges (approximate is fine)
  • Services you do NOT offer
  • Insurance accepted (if applicable)
🤖

AI Personality & Tone

  • How you want your AI to sound (warm, professional, urgent)
  • Name for your AI (or we'll suggest one)
  • Scripts or greetings you'd like used
  • Phrases or tone to avoid
📞

Call Handling Rules

  • Who gets escalated calls (owner, manager, on-call)
  • Emergency vs. routine call definitions
  • How to handle after-hours inquiries
  • Voicemail or text preferences
