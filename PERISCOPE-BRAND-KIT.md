Every day, small and mid-size businesses lose thousands of dollars to:
- Missed calls that go to voicemail (40% are never returned)

The business owner is **underwater** — buried in operations, unable to see what's slipping through the cracks.

A periscope lets you see clearly above the surface while you're submerged.  
That's exactly what we do for businesses.

**You're underwater in operations. We give you eyes above the surface.**

Every call. Every text. Every email. Every booking request.  
Seen. Handled. Never missed.

This isn't just a name — it's the brand promise: **total visibility + total action.**

## PART 4: VISUAL IDENTITY

### Logo Concept

**Primary Mark:** A stylized periscope icon where the tube doubles as a signal/pulse indicator — representing both vision AND communication. The top lens becomes a subtle "eye" or "lens aperture" motif.

**Secondary Mark:** A geometric "P" lettermark where the bowl of the P subtly incorporates a periscope viewing angle (top curve opens upward like a periscope head).

**Wordmark:** PERISCOPE — set in a modern geometric sans, all caps, tracking +100, with the dot above the letter "I" replaced by a small lens or signal pulse icon.

**Logo Versions:**
- Primary: Icon + Wordmark (horizontal)
- Stacked: Icon above Wordmark
- Icon Only: For favicons, app icons, small formats
- White Reverse: For dark backgrounds
- Monochrome: Single color, for embossing/embroidery

**Clear Space Rule:** Minimum clear space = height of the "P" letterform on all sides.

#### Google Fonts (Free Implementation)
- Space Grotesk: `https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Space+Grotesk`
- Inter: `https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter`
- Space Mono: `https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Space+Mono`

#### Typography Rules
- **Headlines:** Left-aligned or centered on hero only. Never right-aligned.
- **Body:** Always left-aligned for readability.
- **Capitalization:** Brand name = PERISCOPE (caps). Headers = Title Case. Body = Sentence case.
- **Line Height:** Headers 1.1–1.2. Body 1.6–1.7.
- **Letter Spacing:** Display headlines +0.02em. Body default.

---

### Iconography & Illustration Style

**Icon Style:** Duotone — Navy base + Teal accent. Rounded corners. 2px stroke weight.
**Illustration Style:** Geometric, minimal, tech-forward. Think: circuit-meets-blueprint aesthetic. No people illustrations — use abstract shapes and data visualizations.

**Photography Direction:**
- Real business settings: dental offices, HVAC trucks, law offices, service counters
- Warm, productive environments — not sterile stock photos
- People looking at phones or tablets (communication moments)
- No cheesy headsets-on-head stock photography
- Color grade: Slightly cooler tones, high contrast, clean

---

### Motion & Animation Principles

- **Entrance animations:** Subtle fade-up (translateY 20px → 0, opacity 0 → 1, 400ms ease-out)
- **CTA hover:** Scale 1.02 + subtle glow in Signal Blue
- **Data counters:** Count up animation on scroll — makes stats feel alive
- **The Periscope Rise:** Logo reveal animation — periscope icon rises up from below frame (brand moment for splash screens, presentations)
- **Loading states:** Pulse animation in Teal — feels like a heartbeat, reinforces "always on"

## PART 5: BRAND VOICE & MESSAGING

### Brand Personality

| Dimension | Position |
|---|---|
| Formal ←→ Casual | 30% toward casual — smart but accessible |
| Simple ←→ Complex | 25% toward complex — we explain tech in plain language |
| Serious ←→ Playful | 20% toward playful — confident with a hint of wit |
| Reserved ←→ Expressive | 40% toward expressive — we're proud of results |

### The 4 Voice Traits

#### 1. SHARP
We cut through jargon. Every sentence earns its place. No filler.
- ✅ "Your AI is live in 48 hours."
- ❌ "We provide a streamlined onboarding experience to help you leverage our platform effectively."

#### 2. CONFIDENT
We know what we do and we do it well. No hedging.
- ✅ "Periscope handles it."
- ❌ "Periscope might be able to help with some of those challenges."

#### 3. HUMAN
We're AI, but we talk like a trusted business partner — not a press release.
- ✅ "Missed calls cost you more than you think. Let's fix that."
- ❌ "Revenue leakage associated with missed inbound calls represents a significant operational challenge."

#### 4. FORWARD
We're always pointing at the outcome — the future state, not the feature.
- ✅ "Your customers get answered. You get back to work."
- ❌ "Our platform features AI-powered call routing capabilities."
