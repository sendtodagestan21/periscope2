FAQ

Common questions.

Why is there an onboarding fee? +
Configuring your AI takes real work — our team writes your scripts, sets up call routing, builds your intake flow, and runs test calls before you go live. The $500 onboarding fee covers that labor. It's non-refundable because the work is done whether you continue or not — and it stays done no matter what.
What does $500/month actually cover? +
Your ongoing $500/month covers active call handling, the AI platform and infrastructure, your dashboard, monthly performance reviews, and script/routing updates as your business changes. It's a fully managed service — not just software access.
Do I need to change my phone system or install anything? +
No. Company Periscope works alongside your existing phone number and setup. Nothing to install, no hardware, no changes your staff needs to make. We route calls through your current system — the transition is invisible to your team and customers.
What exactly does the 30-day guarantee cover? +
If you're not satisfied within 30 days of going live, we'll refund your $500 subscription fee — the first month included in your $1,000 payment. The $500 onboarding fee is not refundable. To request a refund, email us within 30 days of your go-live date. That's it.
Can I cancel after the first month? +
Yes, anytime. There are no contracts and no cancellation fees. Let us know before your next billing date and you won't be charged again. We'd rather have clients who are staying because they're getting value — not because they're locked in.
How long until my AI is live and handling calls? +
48 hours from your onboarding call — sometimes faster. We'll reach out within 24 hours of your payment to schedule the call, and from there it typically takes one business day to configure and go live.
