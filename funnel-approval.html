From curious stranger to paying client — in 5 stages.

Each stage is designed to deliver value before asking for anything. By the time a prospect reaches the offer, they've already gotten something useful from us — and they understand exactly what they're getting into.

A prospect lands on one of our three opt-in pages. They enter their email to get the Calculator result, request the Stress Test, or download the Script Pack. This is the first value exchange — we give something genuinely useful, they give us their contact info.

We offer a genuine 2-day free trial of Company Periscope — no credit card required. This removes all perceived risk and lets the product do the selling. During the trial, the AI receptionist handles their actual calls, and the business owner sees firsthand how it works. At the end of day 2, they get an email with their trial results and a link to activate the paid pilot.

Why It Works

The psychology behind every stage.

This funnel isn't built on pressure or urgency tactics — it's built on genuine value delivery at every stage. Each step earns the right to ask for the next one.

💡
The Calculator Makes the Problem Real
Abstract problems don't convert. Dollar amounts do. When a dental practice owner sees "$8,400/month in missed revenue" with their actual call volume entered, the problem stops being hypothetical. They feel it. That's what makes them want a solution.
🤝
The Trial Removes All Risk
Most B2B SaaS requires a demo, a proposal, a contract, a card. We flip the model — try it first, pay after. This works especially well for local business owners who are skeptical of tech tools and have been burned before. The 2-day trial converts to paid pilots at a much higher rate than cold pitches.
🔢
$297 Is Below the Decision Threshold
For most small practices, anything under $300 can be approved without involving a partner or running it by an accountant. The pilot priced at $297 is deliberate — it's just low enough to be a "yes" decision, while still being high enough to signal real commitment from the prospect.
📈
The Pilot Creates Product Stickiness Before the Ask
By the end of the 7-day pilot, the business owner has seen what life looks like with Company Periscope. Missed calls are being handled. Patients are being followed up with. Turning it off now feels like a loss, not a choice. That's when the subscription conversation is easiest.
