Emails 2–5 follow the same structure as the calculator sequence with minor framing adjustments. Copy the Calculator sequence for emails 2–5 and adapt the first line of each email to reference the stress test instead.

  • Tag to add on trigger: opt-in-stresstest
  • Assign to team member: Internal task to conduct the stress test within 24 hours of opt-in
  • Internal notification: Send team email/SMS when this form is submitted so the audit doesn't get missed

Subject Got it — your practice audit is scheduled, {{contact.first_name}}
Preview Text We'll contact your office within 24 hours. Here's what to expect.
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, We've received your request for the free Response Time Stress Test. Here's what happens next: Within the next 24 hours, our team will contact your practice as a prospective new patient. We'll call, and — if you requested it — also test your text and web inquiry response. We document everything: how many rings before answer, whether calls are missed entirely, how long text responses take, what after-hours looks like. All of it. Within 24–48 hours of the test, you'll receive a private PDF report with our full findings — including a revenue impact estimate based on what we observed. One thing to know: we won't identify ourselves as Company Periscope when we call. That's the only way to get an accurate picture of what a real new patient experiences. Keep an eye on your inbox. Your report is coming. — The Company Periscope Team
