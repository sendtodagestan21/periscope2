## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**The opportunity:** Company Periscope is a fast-deploy AI receptionist with clear, measurable ROI — every missed call has a dollar value. The product isn't limited to dental; it wins anywhere inbound call volume drives revenue and front desk capacity is a bottleneck.

**My recommendation:** Don't spread thin across all industries on day one. Win fast in 2–3 high-ROI verticals using a tight paid + outbound combo, then scale what works.

**The 50-signup number is achievable in 30 days** with the right targeting, an optimized funnel, and roughly **$15,000–$22,000 in campaign spend.**

---

## PART 1: TARGET AUDIENCE SELECTION

### Why These 3 Verticals First

I analyzed the product fit, call volume, average ticket size, and decision-maker accessibility across all viable industries. These three have the shortest path to conversion:

---

### Vertical 1: Dental Practices ⭐ PRIMARY

**Why they convert fastest:**
- Average value of a new patient: $800–$3,000 lifetime
- Dental offices get 40–80 inbound calls/day — all revenue-critical
- Most run with 1–2 front desk staff who are constantly overwhelmed
- Decision maker = practice owner or office manager — reachable by phone/LinkedIn/FB
- HIPAA awareness means they trust "purpose-built" solutions over generic tools
- Proven existing use case on companyperiscope.com

**Pain points to hit:**
- "Our phones ring during procedures and we miss calls"
- "We're losing new patient inquiries to voicemail"
- "My front desk can't handle overflow during peak hours"
- "We're paying for a receptionist we barely need after 5pm"

**Target persona:** Dr. Owner (35–55), Office Manager (28–50)
**Target practice size:** 1–3 locations, 1–5 operatories
**Decision timeline:** Fast — if pain is active, they sign same week

---

### Vertical 2: HVAC & Home Services ⭐ HIGH PRIORITY

**Why they convert:**
- Emergency call volume is their entire business — missed = lost job
- Average HVAC job: $200–$5,000
- After-hours calls represent 30–40% of their revenue
- Owners answer their own phones — they HATE this
- $500/month is less than one missed emergency call
- "I'm on the roof and can't answer" is a universal pain point

**Pain points to hit:**
- "I miss calls when I'm on a job"
- "I lose customers when I don't answer on weekends"
- "I need someone to answer and book jobs without me picking up"

**Target persona:** Owner-operator (30–55), small dispatch team
**Target business size:** 1–10 trucks, $500K–$5M revenue
**Decision timeline:** Very fast — emotional, personal pain

---

### Vertical 3: Law Firms & Legal Practices ⭐ HIGH VALUE

**Why they convert:**
- Average new client value: $2,000–$50,000+
- Missing one intake call can cost more than a year of the subscription
- Firms are notoriously bad at after-hours coverage
- Compliance and confidentiality messaging seals trust
- Solo/small firm attorneys can't afford a full-time receptionist

**Pain points to hit:**
- "Potential clients call and hang up when they hit voicemail"
- "I'm in court all day and can't handle new intake calls"
- "I need someone to qualify leads before they reach me"

**Target persona:** Solo attorney, small firm partner (30–60)
**Target business size:** 1–10 attorneys
**Decision timeline:** Moderate — needs trust-building, converts on demo

---

### Verticals 4–5: Reserve for Week 3+ Scaling

- **Medical Practices** — High value, longer compliance sales cycle
- **Auto Repair** — High call volume, lower ticket, fast decision

## PART 2: MESSAGING STRATEGY

### The Master Message

> **"Every call you miss is a customer your competitor answered."**

This is the single line that drives the campaign. It's:
- Industry-agnostic (works for dental, HVAC, legal, auto)
- Immediately personal and painful
- Actionable (implies a fix exists)

### Message Architecture by Vertical

#### Dental
**Hook:** "Your front desk stepped away. Your new patient didn't."
**Body:** While your team is busy with patients in the chair, new patient calls are hitting voicemail — and those callers are booking with the practice down the street. Company Periscope's AI receptionist answers every call, books appointments, and captures patient info automatically.
**CTA:** Get your AI receptionist live in 48 hours. Try it free.

#### HVAC / Home Services
**Hook:** "You were on a job. They called your competitor."
**Body:** Every unanswered call during a job is $200–$2,000 walking away. Company Periscope answers your calls, books the job, and captures the lead — even at 2am, even when you're on a roof.
**CTA:** Never miss a job call again. Start today.

#### Legal
**Hook:** "They called. Got voicemail. Hired someone else."
**Body:** A missed intake call at a law firm can mean $10,000 in lost revenue. Our AI receptionist answers every call, qualifies the lead, and books consultations — 24/7, even when you're in court.
**CTA:** Try Company Periscope free. Live in 48 hours.

---

### Pricing Messaging (Required in All Touchpoints)

**Starter Plan — Lead with this:**
> "Start for $1,000. Just $500/month after. 300 minutes included."

**The ROI framing (always pair with pricing):**
> "One captured call per month covers the cost. Everything else is profit."

**Growth Plan — For businesses with higher call volume:**
> "$1,500 to start, $1,000/month. 1,000 minutes included."

**Fast deploy angle — use everywhere:**
> "Onboarded and live in 48 hours. No tech team required."

## PART 4: FUNNEL STRUCTURE

```
AWARENESS
  ↓ Google Search / Meta Ad / LinkedIn Outreach
LANDING PAGE (vertical-specific)
  ↓ "See how it works" or "Book a demo"
DEMO / JORDAN AI (live conversation)
  ↓ Converts skeptics — they hear the AI in action
OFFER PAGE
  ↓ Pricing clear: $1,000 start / $500/mo — "No risk, cancel anytime"
CHECKOUT + ONBOARDING FORM
  ↓ Immediate: payment + setup form
ONBOARDING (48 hours)
  ↓ AI built, tested, call forwarding set
LIVE → HAPPY CUSTOMER → REFERRAL
```

### Funnel Optimization Notes

1. **The demo is the close.** Jordan (the AI) should be front-and-center on the landing page. "Talk to Jordan" converts skeptics faster than any copy.
2. **Vertical-specific landing pages** outperform generic by 2–3x. Build dental, HVAC, and legal pages separately.
3. **Pricing clarity eliminates friction.** Show the math: "$500/month ÷ one new patient = paid for."
4. **Urgency without desperation:** "We onboard in 48 hours — your calls are ringing right now."
